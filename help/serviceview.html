illuminate Services View

The illuminate services view allows you to see which services (managed by SMF, the Service Management Facility) are running on your system.

The left hand pane shows the services that are running, either as a simple linear list or as a tree view. The tree view is based solely on the service names, and implies no relationship or dependency between services.

The right hand pane shows three tabs:

  • Status - shows whether the service is running, and if so, the processes associated with the service.
  • Dependencies - lists services that the service depends on, and which services depend on the service.
  • Log file - if available, the log file associated with the service can be accessed.
